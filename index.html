28 minutes
Updated May 5, 2025

In this lesson, we'll explore the core principles of responsive design and how to implement them in your web projects. You'll learn about fluid grids, flexible images, media queries, and mobile-first approach. By the end of this lesson, you'll understand how to create websites that provide an optimal viewing experience across a wide range of devices.
